Biography

Being a Grand Rapids native, I am a bit biased when I say that Grand Rapids is a wonderful place to live! The combination of small-town feel and big-city opportunity is hard to beat. Since I have been born and raised in this wonderful city, I know about many of the small treasures that Grand Rapids has to offer--including the city's best new restaurants and greatest coffee shops. And of course, being a Realtor, I pride myself in knowing all about the neighborhoods in Grand Rapids and surrounding areas.


I grew up in a family that was very involved in the real estate industry. Because of this, real estate has been an interest and passion of mine for as long as I can remember. I absolutely love my job, and I've been told that my passion shines through in all of my real estate endeavors.


My goal is to make your real estate journey as seamless and hassle-free as possible. Whether you're looking to buy, sell, build, or invest in property, I'm here to help your dreams become a reality. From the beginning until the end, I will be in constant communication with you, and be by your side every step of the way (through closing and beyond). I will listen to your story, and create a plan that best meets your desires. Whether you're a young couple looking for your first home, or a seasoned real estate investor, my goal is to meet your own personal needs and unique situation.


As your real estate agent, you gain access to my vast network of service providers. You can expect promptness, honesty, integrity, and superb communication from me throughout the entire process. In addition, with my strong yet fair negotiation style, my clients feel very well-served.


If you're looking for a Realtor who can help you reach your real estate goals, I would love to hear from you and get an idea of how my services can best fit your needs!
